How ranking works?

Only your top10 hardest routes/problems are calculated.

6a gives you 400 points, 6b=500, 6c=600, 7a=700...8a=1000 points.
Toproping decuts your points by 150.

Ranking changes nightly, so don't expect your ranking to change immediately after a tick =)
