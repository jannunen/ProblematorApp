Problemator is brought to you by Cave Oy ©2008-{$smarty.now|date_format:"%Y"}


The main idea for the software is to offer ways to track your climbing in indoors.

Indoors problems change often and when the problem is gone, it's gone. So the conventional ways to track ascents is not viable solution. This is where Problemator comes into picture.
